World Builder

Before time there was nothing, and before nothing, there were monsters.

In the void the eternal creatures squirmed and writhed in a ceaseless mass with little to no room between each other. There was no conflict until one of the ceaseless chaoses wanted space and slew another. The blood of the beast spread rapidly and as it did the bodies of the eternals drifted apart. The void was no longer empty and packed with entities, it was now flooded with elemental chaos. The swirting chaos coalesced into into the elements fire, earth, life, water, metal, light, wind, and void. These elements began to draw together, the elements that were unable to coalesce continued to swirl mixed together on the outskirts of the elemental plains.
